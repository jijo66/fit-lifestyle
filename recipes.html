Recipe of the week: Ugali and sukuma wiki(kales) with meat

This is a very low fat,high protein, high vitamin and iron meal. Ugali is easy to cook and this meal can be ready in as little as 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 cups white maize meal
  • 1 kales
  • 1 onion
  • 1 large beef cube
  • 2 tomatoes
  • beef cut in small pieces(optional)
  • pepper(optional)

Cooking time:30 min

serves:2-4

cooking instructions

Ugali:
  1. boil the water
  2. Add the maize meal slowly and stir
  3. Stir some more. Turn down the heat
  4. continue until it turns into a solid mass
  5. Turn the pot upside down on a plate until the Ugali falls out.
Sukuma wiki:
  1. cut up the onion, tomato and 1 garlic clove
  2. Remove stems from the sukuma wiki, rinse the leaves and cut them up into small pieces
  3. In a large frying pan, fry the onions and garlic(if any).
  4. Add the meat. When brown add the sukuma wiki and the tomato
  5. Add 2 cups of water and bring to boil and simmer for about half an hour. Add spices as desired
  6. Serve the sukuma with the ugali
