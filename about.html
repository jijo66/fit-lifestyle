What are we about?

Simply put:Fitness

This site aims to inspire you to take up new challenges, to set and destroy your fitness goals and to motivate you to get moving. We constantly update the website to keep you informed on whats new in the fitness industry and the latest fitness news and breakthroughs in order to help you live a healthy fulfilled and fun life!

Our mission

  1. To provide a platform for Kenyans to meet and encourage one another in their quest to loose weight and live healthier fitter lives
  2. To Provide a platform that will provide information to the public about the importance of observing good nutritional and diet habits
  3. To Provide a platform for Kenyans to learn and practice various workouts and exercises in order for them to be physically active

Our Vision

To see an obese free Kenya and to make Kenyans see that working out can be fun and healthy food tasty.
