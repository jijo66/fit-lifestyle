Good Nutrition is the bane of living a healthy, happy and fulfilled life!

You can work out all you want but if what you are putting into your mouth is garbage you are likely to not get any results.

Here at fit lifestyle we believe nutrition is one of the most important factor that needs to be considered if obesity is to be fought. To help you stay on track we have a recipes section that highlights some healthy kenyan recipes for you to try. The section is constantly updated so make sure you're constantly checking for the latest dish!

The number one rule for loosing weight in terms of nutrition is simple:Eat smaller potions than you currently do

Leaving a calorie deficit helps your body loose weight as it will use some of its fat reserves. However eating less should not be confused with skipping meals. This is actually dangerous for you and could actually make you gain more weight! Studies have shown that skipping meals slows down your bodies metabolism making your body store fat. Who would have known that skipping a meal can actually make you heavier? The human body is a marvel.
